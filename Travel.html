Travel

How to get to Ios

When travelling to Ios its important to know that there are no direct flights to the island as there is no airport. You need to fly to a neighbouring island and then get a ferry across. Note that ferries vary in price depending on the time of day you go, so try and plan you flights for the cheapest option if you can. You can fly from Bristol and most London airports and would need to fly to either Santorini or Mykynos before getting a ferry to Ios. Please also note that flight for May 2024 aren't out yet, so we are basing all the information on this page from this years flights and ferries. We would recommend buying flights when they are fully released and in the price range of £100 - £200 as the prices fluctuate a lot. Its not always best to buy the flights straight away so wait for them to hit the range above and book then.

When should we be in Ios?

We would advise you being in Ios from Wednesday - Wednesday (29th May - 5th June), as we have lots of things planned to do in a big group (only if you want to). But as a bare minimum would advise people to be there from Thursday 30th - Monday 3rd , just incase the wedding is moved +/- 1 day in the unlikely event of bad weather.

Transfers/Ferries

As mentioned before, the only way to get from Santorini and Mykonos to Ios is by ferry transfer. Please note that the ferry prices differ quite a lot depending on what time of day you go. For example early morning you can get a ferry for around 10 eruos per person, but for the rest of the day it would be 60 euros per person. This is the link the where you book the ferries. Ferryscanner.com If your flight arrives early and you ferry isn't for a few hours to Ios you can get a taxi/bus (bus is so much cheaper) into the town to kill some time and then get a taxi/bus to the port for when your ferry is due. (don't be late as they don't wait at all) Or... You could stay overnight in either Santorini or Mykonos and get and early ferry. Or go straight to the Port from the airport, theres shops and cafes at the port if you want to wait there for your ferry

Transport on the island

Bus I your best friend. Taking the local shuttle buses is the cheapest and easiest way to get round ios, Santorini and mukynos. Its roundly 2 erous one way, so just be careful of scammers in the port trying to make you pay 50 euros from the city to the port as it not the right bus you should be getting on. In Ios there is a shuttle bus that runs every half hour or so to the 3 main areas. Also there is cars, buggies, quads and peds to hire if you'd prefer that. Just make sure you take your driving license and that the photo is in date.
