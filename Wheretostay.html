Where to stay
Staying in Ios

For the wedding you can stay on the island of Ios. There's plenty of hotels and villas whether you are looking to stay on your own, with someone or in a group.

We recommend you get planning as early as possible as hotels and villas will quickly book up.

Ios has everything you need on the island, and would make the perfect stay, whether its for the weekend or if you plan to make a holiday of it and stay longer
