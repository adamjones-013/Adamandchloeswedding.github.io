  • Never bay
    Never bay is a hidden rocky cove on the other side of the Island. Again we plan to do a day trip here and it would be good if everyone would join together to do it on the same day with us. Its about a 20 minute hike over some rocky cliffs but its worth it when you get there. Theres flat rocky areas to sit and some rock jumps. The only way to get to the far side of the island is by hiring a car, quad or mo-ped, so bare that in mind and its about a 30 minute drive. At the start of the hike theres a restaurant, beach and shop for anything you may need.

Bars/Clubs

  • Pathos Club and Restaurant
    Pathos is a club, bar, and restaurant located on the island of Ios. It is a venue where guests can enjoy refreshing cocktails, great music by various DJs, and various gastronomic suggestions at the restaurant. Moreover, Pathos often hosts important events, such as wedding parties and celebrations.
  • Scorpion
    Scorpion is the after-club of Ios perched on a cliff just outside the main town. The bar is housed in a round cave-like building, rather small but very special.
