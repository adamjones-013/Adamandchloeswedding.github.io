Day of the wedding
  • Day before the wedding
    Almas sunset bar @ 7:00pm

    The day before the wedding we have made a reservation at the Sunset Bar Almas. It would be good for everyone who can to join us for a chilled drink and meet up with everyone an see the sunset views over the island

  • The wedding day
    Cycladic gem luxury villa @ 4:00pM

    Please arrive at the villa from 4:00pm but no later than 4:30pm. We will be providing transfers to the wedding which will pick up from the 3 main locations, Chora, Mylopotas and the Port.

  • Day after the wedding
    harmony Mexican tapas and bar @ 4:00pM

    We also have a reservation for food and drinks at a Mexican tapas place. You don't have to come but it has some of the best food on the island, happy hour drinks, rock jumps and the beach right next to it and live music/dj. Hopefully you can join us there if youre not too hungover

  • Other days after the wedding

    If you are staying for a few days after the wedding, we have a few things that we would like to do, and would love if other would join us in a big group in exploring beaches, going out for drinks and food in places we know you would love.
